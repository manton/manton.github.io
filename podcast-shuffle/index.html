Manton Reece » Podcast Shuffle
Random individual podcasts delivered to your podcast client or newsreader.

Podcast Shuffle is an RSS feed with randomly selected podcasts. Each item is a direct link to the most recent enclosure for a particular show. The feed only has one item, so when you subscribe with a newsreader such as NetNewsWire 2.0, you won't have to download multiple MP3s at once.

» Subscribe now (RSS)

The feed is updated several times throughout the day with new podcasts. If you would prefer to receive only one new podcast a day, set your newsreader preferences to only refresh the feed every 24 hours.

Podcasts are currently chosen randomly from the full directory at iPodder.org. Some filtering is applied to restrict results (English only, maximum file size 40MB).
